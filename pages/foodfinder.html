Food Finder

Angular, Javascript, AJAX, Bootstrap, CSS

View Repo

Can't decide what to eat tonight? Food Finder will give you a selection of randomized food and cocktail recipes. This SPA also gives a recommendation of the top three rated restaurants based off the geolocation of the device.

Powered by Zomato, Food2Fork, and CocktailDB APIs.
