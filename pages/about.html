ABOUT


By taking a holistic approach, I am able to deliver a more meaningful product—this means considering its technology, offline interactions, user experience, and societal impact. I am excited by unique business problems, and enjoy the cycle of prototyping and testing possible solutions. Because most people are not aware of what they want, it is my goal to consistently ship products that users didn't know they needed.
